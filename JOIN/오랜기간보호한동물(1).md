## **오랜 기간 보호한 동물(1)**

🔒 [문제 보기](https://programmers.co.kr/learn/courses/30/lessons/59044)

🔑 풀이 답안 - Oracle

```SQL
SELECT *
FROM   (SELECT name, 
               datetime 
        FROM   animal_ins 
        WHERE  animal_id NOT IN (SELECT animal_id 
                                 FROM   animal_outs) 
        ORDER  BY datetime) 
WHERE  ROWNUM <= 3; 
```
